Suvarnaprashan: Nurturing Your Child's Future

What is Suvarnaprashan?

Ancient Ayurvedic Practice
Daily or periodic administration of purified gold (Suvarna bhasma) mixed with honey and medicated ghee to children.
Recommended Age
From birth up to 16 years, promoting overall health and development.

Key Benefits

  • Boosts immunity & prevents infections.
  • Enhances memory, intellect & concentration.
  • Improves digestion & metabolism.
  • Supports healthy physical growth.
  • Promotes overall vitality & complexion.
  • Reduces recurrence of allergic conditions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the ideal age for Suvarnaprashan?

A: Suvarnaprashan can be administered from birth up to 16 years of age.

Q: How often should Suvarnaprashan be given?

A: It is recommended daily, or at least monthly on Pushya Nakshatra days for maximum benefits.

Q: Are there any side effects?

A: When prepared and administered correctly, Suvarnaprashan has no known side effects and is safe for children.

Q: What are the main ingredients?

A: It typically contains purified gold bhasma, honey and medicated ghee infused with specific herbs.

Q: Can it be given with other medicines?

A: Yes, it is generally safe to administer alongside other conventional medications; consult your pediatrician for specific cases.
