What is Suvarnaprashan?

Suvarnaprashan is an ancient Ayurvedic practice involving the periodic administration of purified gold (Suvarna bhasma) mixed with honey and medicated ghee to children. It is recommended for children from birth up to 16 years to promote overall health and development.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q: What is the ideal age for Suvarnaprashan?

    A: Suvarnaprashan can be administered from birth up to 16 years of age.

    Q: How often should it be given?

    A: It can be given daily or at least once a month on Pushya Nakshatra days for a minimum of 6 months.

    Q: Are there any side effects?

    A: When prepared and administered correctly, as per Ayurvedic texts, there are no known side effects.
